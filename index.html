Crisis Protocol

Hard Stop - Follow these 4 steps in order.

1 Stop immediately.

No negotiating. Close your eyes, take a deep breath, and disengage from internal work right now.

2 Ground the body.

Orient slowly and name 5 things you see. Press your feet into the floor. Lengthen your exhale. Move or hum.

3 Contact a human.

Call a trusted person or a crisis line: 988 (US/CAN), 13 11 14 (AUS), 116 123 (UK), or findahelpline.com.

4 Hard Stop for 24 hours.

No parts work for at least 24 hours. Low-stakes activities only.

Session Complete

Great work today. Remember the 72-hour rule: favor low-stakes activities while your system integrates this work. Ensure you have copied your transcript.
